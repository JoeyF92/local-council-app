Shape the Future of Our Community

Here you have the power to shape our community by reviewing new submissions and making decisions that will impact the future. Your role is crucial in ensuring that only the most promising proposals proceed to the public voting stage.
As you assess each submission, carefully consider its alignment with our council's values and objectives. Remember, any approved projects will be open for public voting, so it's essential to adhere to the council's format when making edits or modifications.
Additionally, you can access the current results for the month, enabling informed decision-making and helping prioritize submissions that have gained public support. Your dedication and commitment to this process play a vital role in transforming ideas into reality and making our community an even better place to live. Thank you for your invaluable service!
