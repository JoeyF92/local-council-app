Welcome to YourVote

Welcome to our local council app! Here, you have the power to make a difference in our community.

Every month, we present a list of proposals that aim to improve our area. You have 7 votes at your disposal to cast on the proposals that resonate with you the most. Feel free to distribute your votes among multiple proposals or concentrate them on a few you believe in strongly. Your votes count! But it doesn't end there. Once the voting period concludes, we'll announce the approved proposals that will be implemented. You can follow the progress of these projects as they come to life, making a tangible impact on our surroundings. Additionally, we share success stories of past projects, showcasing how the collective efforts of our community have led to positive changes. Join us in shaping the future of our local area through dotmocracy-style voting and witnessing the transformative power of your participation. Together, we can create a vibrant and thriving community we are proud to call home.
